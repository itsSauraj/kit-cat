# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive community.

- Be kind and considerate.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Do not harass or discriminate.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Report unacceptable behavior to maintainers.

Enforcement is at the discretion of the maintainers. Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainers.
